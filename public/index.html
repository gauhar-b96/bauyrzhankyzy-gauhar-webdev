Project - Poetry Paradise

Besides working on weekly assignments, we had to come up with our own project and build the web application for it. I enjoy composing poems, but since it is such an “old-fashioned” thing to do and the community of modern poets is so small, there are not that many online resources to publish my work for those who would appreciate it. That is how the idea for the Poetry Paradise was born. Poetry Paradise is a web platform that not only allows access to published poetry, but also gives users the ability to post their own compositions, receive feedback, edit masterpieces in progress, translate poems, or simply enjoy reading pieces of art from talented people around the globe.
